Varanasi Bodh Gaya Pind dan

Varanasi Bodh Gaya Pind dan

Varanasi is the oldest pilgrimage destination on the banks of the river ganga which is located in Uttar Pradesh, India. Varanasi is a city which is visited by people from all over the world irrespective of the season. Varanasi is also known as Kashi; this is the spiritual capital of India. And every Hindu tend to visit this destination once in a lifetime. You will find many temples along the banks of the holy river Ganga. Nearby to Varanasi is Bodhgaya which is famous for Buddha temples, Vishnu temple, Bodhi tree and others.

YOUR ITINERARY (DAY WISE)

Day-1

Day 1: Varanasi

Our representative will pick up from the Airport/Railway station and check in to Hotel. After having breakfast Rest of the day until evening is free for leisure activities, shopping or sightseeing that you desire. Right before sunset get ready to experience one of the most memorable moments of your life, as you will be taken to River Ganges. Enjoy Ganga Arti at the holy ghats of Kashi. Get a glimpse of the spiritualism at the Ganga Ghats (river front). You can also enjoy the Ganga Arti by boat (optional and by own cost). Night stay at hotel in Varanasi.

    Day-2

    Day 2: Varanasi – Bodhgaya

    Early morning, we will go for a boat ride on the Ganges (optional and by own cost). It is a mystical and spiritual experience as you watch people offering water to the Sun God and devotees taking holy dip in the Ganges. We will visit all the famous Ghats of Varanasi via boat. Later visit the most religious Kashi Vishwanath temple, Annapurna Temple, the Bharat Mata temple, Sankat Mochan Mandir , Manas Mandir and BHU (Banaras Hindu university). Return to hotel for breakfast. After breakfast, we will drive to Bodhgaya will take 6hrs to reach. Check in to a hotel overnight stay at hotel in Bodhgaya.

      Day-3

      Day 3: Bodhgaya

      After having breakfast, we will drive to Gaya which is 12 km away from Bodhgaya. When we reached Gaya perform the pind dan and other rituals activities. After all the rituals activities then we visit famous and unique temples Vishnu pad temple where you can see the footprint of lord Vishnu and then we visit Vishnu temple, Chinese temple, Tibetan temple, Japanese temple, Phalguna river. By evening we will return back to Bodhgaya. Overnight stay at hotel in Bodhgaya.

        Day-4

        Day 4: Bodhgaya drop to Airport Railway station

        After Breakfast, go explore the streets on your own. Later you will be dropped to the airport/Railway station.
