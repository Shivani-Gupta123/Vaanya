Manimahesh Yatra Tour Package

Manimahesh Yatra Tour Package

Mani Mahesh Trek is one of the major pilgrimage sites and a popular trekking destination in Himachal Pradesh. Manimahesh, also known as Chamba Kailash, is one of the Panch Kailash situated in the Pir Panjal range of the Bharmour subdivision of Himachal’s Chamba district. It is believed that Lord Shiva created his abode here after his marriage to Mata Parvati. The peak is a rock formation in the shape of Shivling that stands high over Manimahesh Lake. The snowfield at the base of the mountain is called Shiva’s Chaugan. Both of these places are found sacred and are visited by many Hindu pilgrims.
