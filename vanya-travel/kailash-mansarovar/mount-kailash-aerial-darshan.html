Kailash Mansarovar Aerial Darshan 2024-2025

The Kailash Mansarovar Yatra is considered as the great Tirtha and the ultimate pilgrimage destination by the followers of Hindu religion. Mt Kailash is believed to be the place of immense spiritual and cultural significance. As per the Hindu Mythologies, the Kailash Parvat is the great mountain where Mahadev resides with Maa Parvati and other devta gan.
The Kailash Mansarovar region has a distinctive scenic splendor, rich cultural legacy, and ethereal ambiance. Mount Kailash is believed to be the centre of the universe. For years, Kailash Mansarovar has been attracting devotees from all over the world. The journey to Kailash Mansarovar has the ability to atone for our shortcomings and cleanse us of our sins. Sighting Mount Kailash once in a lifetime even from a distance is considered to give immense satisfaction to Bhole bhakts. According to the Hindu Mythology, the lake Mansarovar was first created by Lord Brahma in his mind, after which it was materialized on Earth. Thus in Sanskrit it is known as ‘Manasarovar’ which is the combination of words ‘Manasa Sarovaram’ which means ‘mind’ and ‘lake’. The lake is also known for its summer abode for Hamsa or swan. These birds are considered as sacred in the Hindu Dharma and an important element in the symbology of the subcontinent, representing wisdom and beauty.
