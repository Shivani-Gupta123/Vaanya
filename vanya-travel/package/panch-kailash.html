Panch Kailash

Panch Kailash Yatra comprises the journey to five Kailash are Mt. Kailash in Tibet, Adi Kailash in Uttarakhand, Kinnaur Kailash, Manimahesh Kailash and Shrikhand Kailash are in Himachal Pradesh.Thousands of Shiva devotees travel to these Kailash peaks in hopes of receiving Bhole Baba's blessings. It is said that the Panch Kailash Yatra offers happiness, salvation, and spiritual release. Since these Panch Kailash are believed to be the  five abodes of Lord Shiva it is said where there is Kailash there is Gauri kund which is also the bathing Kund of Mata Parvati signifying the togetherness of Lord Shiva and Mata Parvati.
