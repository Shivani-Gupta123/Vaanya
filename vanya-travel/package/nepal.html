Nepal Tour Packages

Nepal is a destination of exploration and unforgettable adventures, with its spectacular landscapes and rich old cultures. Nepal provides a wide range of activities like hiking, mountaineering, rafting, elephant poling, and micro-light flights over the Himalayas. The country has 15 National & Wildlife Parks, including two UNESCO Heritage sites. In addition, the country is home to Lumbini, the birthplace of Gautama Buddha, and Mount Everest, the highest mountain peak in the world. Adventure travel and mountaineering are some among the most sought-after vacation spots. Nepal is a religiously diverse culture where the main faith is Hinduism. Kathmandu is home to the Pashupatinath Temple, one of the most important Hindu religious sites in the world. Swargadwari, Lake Gosainkunda, Devghat, Manakamana, Pathibhara, and Mahamrityunjaya Shivasan Nepal are a few more Hindu pilgrimage destinations.Another significant religion is Buddhism, or Swayambhunath, the Monkey temple is one more well-known Buddhist site in Kathmandu. With seven World Heritage Sites located within a 7-kilometre radius, Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal, is a culturally rich place. Galleries in Kathmandu feature both contemporary and historical art, and museums have extensive collections of art, archeological, and historical significance. Offering a window into Nepal's rich and culturally diverse society, the Kathmandu Valley is a popular tourist destination.With the help of our packages, you may experience every aspect of Nepal, from thrilling activities in Pokhara to holy sightseeing in Kathmandu, from stunning mountain peaks to the charming twin valley. Nepal is the centre of all tourism-related activities.
