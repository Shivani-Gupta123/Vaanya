Amarkantak Maihar Shakti Peeth Tour

'Maihar' one of the Shakti Peeths meaning 'Mother's Necklace', the name to this place has as deep relevance to its existance. Maihar is located on Trikuta Mountain of Stana Madhya pradesh. It is the place where Neclace of Maa Sati fell here when her body got scattered into 51 pieces by Sudharshan Chakra forming the 51 Shakti Peeths. There are 1063 steps to reach the  Maihar Temple or Maa Sharda Devi Temple. it si the only Temple in India dedicated to Maa Sharda Devi. When visiting Maihar Temple do talk to the local padits to know about the mysteries of the temple and about the biggest devotee Alha who is still believed to visit the temple and do first Shringar of Sharda Mai.
